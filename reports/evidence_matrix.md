# Evidence Matrix

This evidence matrix is used to track which hypotheses are supported, weakened, or still unverified. User-provided screenshots are treated as research leads only and must be source-verified before being used as evidence.

| Hypothesis ID | Hypothesis | Evidence Needed | Source Type | Priority | Status | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| H1 | Short-term financial pressure remains | Revenue growth, margins, EPS, inventory, guidance, and commentary | Nike Investor Relations, SEC filings, earnings call transcripts, reputable financial news | High | Not collected | Test whether near-term pressure remains visible in verified operating data. |
| H2 | Wholesale and Amazon channel recovery may support Nike's turnaround | Wholesale trend, NIKE Direct trend, digital trend, Amazon evidence, retailer commentary, inventory turnover | Nike Investor Relations, SEC filings, Reuters / AP / CNBC, official Amazon / Nike pages, reliable retailer data | High | Not collected | Treat channel recovery as a testable operating hypothesis, not a confirmed turnaround signal. |
| H3 | Football / World Cup demand may be a medium-term catalyst | Football demand signals, category commentary, kit availability, regional performance, sell-through data | Nike official product pages, Nike Investor Relations, Reuters / AP / CNBC, reliable retailer data, demand proxies used carefully | Medium | Not collected | Popularity signals must be verified and should not automatically be treated as revenue proof. |
| H4 | Germany DFB deal may strengthen long-term football brand power in Europe from 2027 onward | Official partnership details, timing, duration, demand evidence, Europe trend, competitive response | DFB official statement, Nike official statement, Reuters / AP, sports business publications, Nike Investor Relations if mentioned | Medium | Not collected | Treat as a strategic brand catalyst hypothesis, not immediate financial proof. |
| H5 | Nike Mind may improve innovation perception, but financial impact remains unproven | Product launch details, availability, sell-through, reviews, management comments, category impact if disclosed | Nike official product pages, Nike press releases, reputable product reviews, Nike Investor Relations, reliable market data | Medium | Not collected | Innovation narrative must be separated from proven financial contribution. |
| H6 | Valuation re-rating depends on earnings recovery | Valuation multiples, EPS trend, revenue quality, margin trend, peer comparison, scenario analysis | Verified market data, SEC filings, Nike Investor Relations, peer filings, reputable financial data sources | High | Not collected | A lower stock price alone is not enough to support a re-rating thesis. |
| H7 | Insider buying may be a supporting confidence signal | Form 4 filings, insider roles, purchase details, holdings, transaction types | SEC Form 4, company filings, source-verified insider transaction databases | Low | Not collected | Use only as supporting context after transaction type and scale are verified. |
| H8 | Competitive pressure remains a key risk | Peer growth, category share, product positioning, market data, innovation comparisons | Competitor annual reports, Reuters / AP / CNBC, sports business sources, verified market data, product pages | High | Not collected | This risk should be tested against verified peer performance and category evidence. |
| H9 | Sponsorship ecosystem may support Nike's long-term brand moat | Verified federation, club, and athlete partnerships, merchandise demand, category sales, visibility evidence | Nike official pages, club / federation official pages, Reuters / AP / CNBC, SEC filings if relevant, reliable sports business sources, official athlete or brand pages | Medium | Not collected | Screenshots remain research leads only until every claimed partnership is verified. |
