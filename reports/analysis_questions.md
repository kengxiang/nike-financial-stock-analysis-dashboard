# Analysis Questions

## Main Business Question
"Is Nike financially healthy, fairly valued, and attractive from an investment perspective?"

## Analysis Hypotheses
- Nike's revenue trend may show whether the business is growing, stable, or slowing over time.
- Profitability and margin patterns may show whether the business is operating efficiently.
- Stock price momentum may show how the market has reacted to Nike's performance and outlook.
- Valuation metrics may help compare Nike's market price with its financial performance.
- Risk indicators may highlight whether strong performance comes with higher uncertainty.

## Evidence Needed
- Verified annual and quarterly financial statement data from official public sources.
- Verified historical stock price data from a reliable market data source.
- Clear source records for every dataset and metric used in the project.
- Validation checks confirming that periods, dates, and metric fields are consistent.
- Supporting notes on any missing data, restatements, or source limitations.
